## GitHub community introduction — English

I’m building **Mawared ERP**, an Arabic-first, multi-company HR and enterprise operations platform.

The current architecture combines an Android application built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose with a TypeScript, Express, Prisma, and PostgreSQL backend. The product scope includes employee management, attendance, leave approval workflows, role-based permissions, reporting, and progressively delivered payroll and procurement capabilities.

I published a public showcase while keeping the commercial source code, credentials, deployment configuration, and operational data private.

Feedback on Arabic RTL experience, HR workflows, permissions, and product architecture is welcome:

## Sharing safety checklist

Before posting screenshots or videos:

- Use demo employees and fictional companies only.
- Hide email addresses, phone numbers, employee IDs, and personal information.
- Hide API URLs, database identifiers, tokens, and browser developer tools.
- Do not publish `.env`, database exports, logs, QR codes, or authentication responses.
- Do not link to the private repository.
- Verify that the screenshot does not show notifications or unrelated personal content.
